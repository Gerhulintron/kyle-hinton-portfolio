Book a Meeting with Kyle

Book a meeting to talk about making an application, website, or other work, to collaborate or to chat about technology or art.

Step-by-Step Booking Guide:

  • Select a Slot: Click on the available date that suits you best in the displayed calendar. Available times for that day will appear—choose the one that fits into your schedule.
  • Confirm Your Time Zone: Calendly automatically detects your time zone to ensure Kyle is synced with your local time. You can manually adjust it if needed.
  • Fill in Your Details: Provide your name, email, and any other requested information so Kyle can prepare for your consultation and send you a meeting link.
  • Pre-Consultation Form: After confirming your meeting, you’ll be prompted to fill out a short form about your project. This helps to maximize the productivity of your session.
  • Confirmation and Reminder: You’ll receive an email confirming your appointment, along with a calendar invite. Calendly will also send you a reminder as the meeting approaches, so it stays top of mind.
  • Reschedule If Needed: If something comes up and you need to change the meeting time, you can do so easily through the confirmation email. Please give at least 24 hours' notice.

Privacy and Security:

Your privacy matters. All information shared through Calendly and during consultations is held in strict confidence.
